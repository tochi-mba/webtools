Keyboard Shortcuts:
Press "p" to pause or play all
Press "c" to close all
Press "m" to mute all
Press "q" to quick switch
Press the up arrow key to increase volume
Press the down arrow key to decrease volume
Press the left arrow key to skip backwards
Press the right arrow key to skip forwards
Double tap on the video to focus
